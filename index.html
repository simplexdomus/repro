Evolutionary Timeline

4.5 Billion Years Ago

Formation of Earth

Planet formation from cosmic dust. Emergence of primordial soup containing organic molecules.

3.8 Billion Years Ago

First Prokaryotes

Emergence of primitive cells without nucleus. Bacteria and archaea dominate the oceans.

2.7 Billion Years Ago

Photosynthesis

Cyanobacteria develop the ability to photosynthesize. Beginning of oxygen accumulation.

2.0 Billion Years Ago

Eukaryotes

Development of complex cells with nucleus through endosymbiosis. Mitochondria become integrated.

1.2 Billion Years Ago

Sexual Reproduction

Meiosis enables genetic recombination. Acceleration of evolutionary diversity.

600 Million Years Ago

Multicellular Organisms

First complex multicellular life forms. Cell differentiation and specialization begins.

540 Million Years Ago

Cambrian Explosion

Explosive diversification of life. Emergence of all modern animal phyla.

Present Day

Modern Complexity

37 trillion cells in the human body. 200+ specialized cell types working in coordination.

Mitosis | Cell Division

Interphase

Cell growth and DNA replication. The cell prepares for division.

Prophase

Chromatin condenses into chromosomes. Nuclear membrane begins to dissolve.

Metaphase

Chromosomes align at the equatorial plane. Spindle fibers attach.

Anaphase

Sister chromatids are pulled to opposite poles.

Telophase

New nuclear membranes form. Cytokinesis divides the cytoplasm.
